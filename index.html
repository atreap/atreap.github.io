ABOUT

Born in San Juan, Puerto Rico. Vivianna spent her childhood and early adult life living in the island. She graduated from Boston University with a degree in French Language and Literature. Vivianna was inpsired by the French artistic expressions, mixed with her tropical island flavor, and her 10+ years of water color traning, allowed her to discover the freedom and intesity of colors in acrylic paints. She began painting for friends and family, experimenting and subsequently impletmenting the use of brushes,knives, and sponges, all while incorporating other mediums and materials such as impasto, gold leaf, sand, salt, and gels for texture. Vivianna currently live and works from her studio in Oklahoma City, Oklahoma.
